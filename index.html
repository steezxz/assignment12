Principles of Gaming

We want to bring the love back into making video games, keeping video games from going pay to win by being outspoken about it on social media, to keep the overall authenticity in games like how it used to be.

Community Driven

We want to bring people together such as in gamers, game developers, designers, artists, and any like-minded people with wanting to keep games from going into the hands of people who just want money off of it without putting any love into it.

Plans & Goals

Our goals for the future is to recruit like-minded people, game developers, designers, and artists all around the world to create video games purely for the love of it and not adding cheap cash grab systems into the game. We believe those cheap cash grab systems deteroriates the game as well as lowers the player base for it as well.
